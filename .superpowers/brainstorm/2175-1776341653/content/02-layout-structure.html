C. Hybrid Rail (Icon + Flyout)

Icon rail by default, expands to labeled sidebar on hover or pin. Best of both worlds -- compact when focused, discoverable when exploring. Like JetBrains IDEs or Slack's recent redesign. Slightly more complex to implement.

Key context from research

Every top competitor (Cursor 3, Windsurf 2.0, Linear, Devin) uses a narrow rail or icon sidebar. The "Agent Command Center" pattern needs maximum horizontal space for session detail (conversation, terminal, diff, DAG). The current Ark sidebar has 12 items -- research recommends consolidating to 7 (merge History into Sessions, group Agents/Flows/Tools into Resources, merge Memory into Knowledge, move Schedules to Settings).
